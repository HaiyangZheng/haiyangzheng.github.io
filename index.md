# üéØ Research Interests
My research focuses on computer vision, with an emphasis on open-world visual recognition. I aim to develop models that can generalize knowledge learned in closed-set settings to dynamic, open-world scenarios. Recently, I am particularly interested in leveraging Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs) for open-world learning and recognition.

### Evolution of My Research Focus

* **Deep Image Clustering (Master‚Äôs Studies)**  
  My research journey began with deep image clustering, with a primary focus on clustering methods driven by data density information.

* **Open-World Category Discovery (PhD Year 1)**  
  Since clustering often admits multiple valid criteria, I shifted my focus toward transferring learned criteria from labeled data to unlabeled data in open-world settings, leading to research on Novel Class Discovery (NCD) and Generalized Category Discovery (GCD).

* **Looking Forward**  
  My future work aims to explore the open-world visual understanding capabilities of multimodal large language models.
